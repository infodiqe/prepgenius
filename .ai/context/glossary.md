# Glossary

- **CTET** — Central Teacher Eligibility Test (CBSE). **Paper II** targets classes VI–VIII.
- **TET / Assam TET** — (State) Teacher Eligibility Test; Assam TET is the beachhead exam.
- **Qualifying exam** — pass/fail certification (CTET); certificate has **lifetime validity** → users pass once and leave. Drives the Season-Pass (not subscription) pricing for CTET.
- **Ranked exam** — recruitment exams (SSC/Banking/Railway) where score → rank → job; these carry recurring-subscription LTV.
- **CDP** — Child Development & Pedagogy (a CTET subject).
- **PYP / Previous-Year Paper** — exact past exam paper; an exam mode.
- **Mock test** — full timed simulation (e.g., CTET 150Q/150min).
- **Weak topic** — topic with low accuracy, surfaced for remediation.
- **Readiness Score** — heuristic composite predicting exam preparedness (pass-probability is future).
- **AI credit** — user-facing unit metering AI usage; backed by a token-cost ledger.
- **Credit ledger** — append-only record of reserve/commit/release/grant/purchase.
- **Draft-only** — AI/extracted content stays Draft until a human approves it.
- **ai_gateway** — the single module that talks to AI providers (Groq→OpenAI→DeepSeek).
- **n8n** — self-hosted automation engine orchestrating reminders.
- **pgvector** — PostgreSQL extension for semantic dedup (no separate vector DB).
- **Season Pass** — one-time, exam-cycle plan ("Crack It").
- **Institution / batch / membership** — multi-tenant coaching-centre model.
- **DPDP** — India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (consent, deletion, minors).
